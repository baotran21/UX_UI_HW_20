Introduction

Our app will help plant lovers and admirers learn how to take better care of the plants they own. They will be able to add their plants into the app and receive suggested basic care information immediately. The app will also give reminders for waterings, soil changings, and sunlight levels. We will offer a community forum feature which will answer plant questions and offer advice. In addition, there will be a searchable database for plants to learn more detailed information.

Problem Statement

We believe that providing regular reminders and notifications for plant care specific to the species will result in plant owners being equipped with knowledge for how to ensure a long and healthy life for their plants.

User Insight Statement

During the survey, we discovered that a large portion/81.8% of participants actually did not have a dedicated plant care schedule. Therefore, we believe that providing them with an application that provides consistent plant care reminders will increase plant longevity and health.
